01 — About

Fresh food.
Future vibes.

ñHASH is a café & kitchen built for the way you actually hang out — good food, great coffee and a neon-lit room that stays buzzing from morning brews to late-night cravings.

Everything is made fresh to order in Kamala Nagar: hot, juicy momos, stacked burgers, loaded fries and a coffee bar that means business. No fuss, no frills — just food that hits.
